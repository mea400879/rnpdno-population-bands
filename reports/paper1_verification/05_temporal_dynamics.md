| Located Dead | 0 | 7 | +0.437 | 0.0003 | * |

Norte is consolidating as a hotspot region for Total, Located Alive, and Located Dead. Located Alive shows the steepest absolute growth (+1.098/yr): from 4 to 17 municipalities, a 4.25x increase. Located Dead emerged from zero to 7 HH municipalities over the study period. Not Located Norte remains flat (8 munis in both Dec 2015 and Dec 2025, slope ns) — suggesting Norte's increase is specifically in resolution outcomes (alive/dead), not in active disappearance reports.
